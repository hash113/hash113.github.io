About Us

Our story

Village Aharam is a pioneer of organic and environmentally conscious healthy cuisine.We are leading and inspiring change by returning to the roots of India, serving lovingly handcrafted and naturally cultivated organic food to nourish hard-working Indians.

How we do it

Handcrafted | natural | organic

Great ingredients deserve great preparation. Morning to night, our skilled crews use traditional cooking techniques on our own organically produced raw materials. It's no coincidence that our managing director is himself a passionate chef. Our recipes are based on Ayurveda and modern Nutri-genomics to get full benefit of health and taste. Our food is cooked on firewood based low-flame . We use steel vessels externally coated with clay that preserve the nutritional value and taste. We never use aluminum in our cooking. Our chapatti flour is ground just before cooking to get the freshest taste. We pluck vegetables from our farm daily so that we can provide you the healthiest and freshest food possible. We serve food in traditional banana leaf and serve water in earthenware. We have created an ambience that is ethnic and village like so that you can feel at home

All our meals are prepared using produce from our own organic farms using traditional cooking techniques. Our recipes are based on Ayurveda and modern Nutri-genomics to get full benefit of health and taste. We have a strong ethical policy of not using artificially fertilized produce or factory reared meat.

Our Farms

Krishi yoga kendra

Our farms are fertilized with compost made from our kitchen waste and animal waste from our own farm animals. We use traditional, natural cow-based farming method known as “Krishi Yoga” where we use panchgavya as the natural fertilizer.

Frequently Asked Questions

" Is organic food better for you ? "

*

The absence of chemical cocktails make the organic food the best one. There is obvious evidence of the harmful influence on human health condition of chemicals. Well balanced soils help to grow healthy food having better taste and containing more nutrient materials.

" Organic food is normally more expensive, how do you keep costs low. ? "

*

All our produce is grown in our farms. We have a team working with our chief environmental scientist. This team works with small local farmers and teaches them the benefits of organic farming. We also source our produce directly from these farmers associated to us. Since we do not have middlemen in between, we manage to keep our costs affordable.
